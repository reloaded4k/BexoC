Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your shipment by visiting our tracking page and entering your tracking number. The system will provide real-time updates on your shipment's location and status.

Shipping times vary depending on the origin and destination countries. Typically, shipments between neighboring African countries take 3-5 business days, while shipments across regions may take 7-10 business days. For expedited shipping options, please contact our customer service.

To file a claim, contact your regional office or email support@bexocargo.com within 7 days of the scheduled delivery date. Include your tracking number, photos of damaged items (if applicable), and a description of the issue. Our claims department will review your case and respond within 48 hours.
