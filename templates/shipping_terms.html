Shipping Terms and Conditions

Last updated: October 1, 2023

1. Scope of Application

These Shipping Terms and Conditions apply to all shipments handled by Bexo Cargo, regardless of the service or mode of transportation used.

By booking a shipment with us, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to be bound by these terms.

2. Booking and Acceptance

All shipments must be booked through our official booking channels, including our website, authorized agents, or customer service.

We reserve the right to refuse, hold, or return any shipment at our sole discretion, particularly if the shipment:

  • Contains prohibited or restricted items
  • Is inadequately packaged
  • Has incorrect or incomplete documentation
  • Poses a safety or security risk

3. Prohibited and Restricted Items

The following items are prohibited from shipping through our services:

  • Illegal substances and contraband
  • Explosives, firearms, and weapons
  • Hazardous or combustible materials
  • Live animals or human remains
  • Counterfeit goods
  • Perishable items without proper packaging

Certain items may be accepted with special handling and proper documentation, including:

  • Alcohol (where legally permitted)
  • Tobacco products
  • Prescription medications (non-narcotic)
  • Valuable items (subject to additional insurance)

4. Packaging Requirements

All shipments must be packaged adequately to protect the contents during transportation, handling, and storage.

Packaging must:

  • Be appropriate for the items being shipped
  • Withstand normal handling during transport
  • Prevent damage to other shipments
  • Include proper cushioning for fragile items
  • Be sealed securely with appropriate tape

We reserve the right to repackage items at the shipper's expense if the original packaging is deemed inadequate.

5. Delivery Times and Delays

While we strive to meet estimated delivery times, these are not guaranteed unless explicitly stated and paid for as a guaranteed service.

We are not liable for delays caused by:

  • Customs clearance procedures
  • Incomplete or inaccurate shipping information
  • Recipient unavailability
  • Weather conditions or natural disasters
  • Transportation network disruptions
  • Government actions or regulations

6. Liability and Insurance

Our liability for loss or damage to shipments is limited to the lesser of:

  • The actual value of the goods
  • $100 USD per shipment
  • The limits established by applicable international conventions

For valuable items, we recommend purchasing additional shipping insurance. Claims for loss or damage must be submitted in writing within 30 days of delivery or expected delivery date.

7. Customs Clearance

The shipper is responsible for providing accurate and complete customs documentation for international shipments.

Bexo Cargo may assist with customs clearance but is not responsible for:

  • Delays caused by customs inspections
  • Duties, taxes, or fees imposed by customs authorities
  • Shipments rejected by customs due to improper documentation

The recipient is responsible for paying any import duties, taxes, and fees unless other arrangements have been made.

8. Tracking and Delivery Confirmation

All shipments receive a unique tracking number that can be used to monitor the shipment's progress.

Delivery confirmation is provided through our tracking system, which may include:

  • Electronic signature capture
  • Recipient name
  • Delivery date and time
