Shipping Terms

Our guidelines and policies for safe and efficient cargo deliveries

Last Updated: January 2025

Prohibited Shipments

Bexocargo strictly prohibits the shipment of the following:

  • Illegal or Contraband Items: Drugs, weapons, counterfeit goods, stolen property, or any item violating local or international laws.
  • Hazardous Materials: Explosives, flammable liquids, toxic substances, or radioactive materials.
  • Restricted Goods: Perishables (without prior approval), live animals, human remains, cash, or high-value items without declared insurance.

Violation Policy:

  • Any prohibited shipment will be confiscated and reported to authorities.
  • The sender will be fully liable for legal consequences and fines.
  • No refunds will be issued for seized or abandoned cargo.

Shipping Rates

All prices are per kilogram (kg) and subject to change based on fuel surcharges or regulatory fees:

Destination Rate (USD/kg)
Within Africa $15

Notes:

  • Minimum chargeable weight: 1 kg.
  • Volumetric weight applies for lightweight, bulky shipments.
  • Additional fees (customs, taxes, storage) are the sender's responsibility.

Required Documentation

The sender must provide:

  • Commercial Invoice (for customs valuation).
  • Packing List (itemized contents, weight, and value).
  • Insurance Proof (for high-value or fragile goods).
  • Health/Safety Certificates (if applicable, e.g., food, pharmaceuticals).
  • Export/Import Permits (for restricted items).

Transit Inspections:

  • Cargo may be unloaded and inspected at transit borders.
  • If documents are missing, the shipment will be delayed until compliance.
  • Sender bears all costs for storage, demurrage, or return shipments.

Liability & Insurance

  • Bexocargo's liability is limited to USD 100 per shipment unless additional insurance is purchased.
  • Senders must declare full value and pay for optional insurance (rate: 2% of declared value).
  • No claims for damages/losses due to improper packaging or prohibited items.

Delays & Compliance

  • Delays caused by missing documents, customs checks, or regulatory holds are the sender's responsibility.
  • Storage fees ($5/day) apply after 48 hours of non-compliance.
  • Bexocargo may abandon shipments after 14 days of non-resolution.
